Our Mission

RIPIN is a nonprofit organization dedicated to helping Rhode Islanders navigate health care, special education, and healthy aging.

Our team of peer professionals have personal experience caring for a loved one with special needs — allowing us to connect with our clients and provide the personal support they need to thrive.

All of our services are free, multilingual, and confidential.

How We Started

Founded in 1991, RIPIN began as a grassroots effort by parents who had personally experienced the challenges of navigating complex systems on behalf of their children with disabilities. What started as a small network of parents sharing information has grown into a statewide organization serving tens of thousands of Rhode Islanders every year.

Today, RIPIN serves as a trusted resource for families across Rhode Island, providing guidance, advocacy, and support across three main areas: special education, health care navigation, and healthy aging.
